FM-DX Maps Lookup
ABOUT
- This is a tool to find a radio station via PI Code. It makes narrowing down a radio station very simple and fast.
- Clicking "Submit!" will open up a new tab to the FM-DX Maps website with the set parameters.

NOTES
- If a station does not come up, it may mean the PI is invalid or a chance the database has not been updated.
- You can leave the Latitude and Longitude fields blank if you only want to look up a valid station.
- Furthermore, you can set your location through the FM-DX Maps site to skip the step of entering your Latitude and Longitude.
- Always make sure to do your research if you are ever uncertain.
